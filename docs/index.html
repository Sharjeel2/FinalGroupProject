The rector of the college is Lieutenant General (Retd) Muhammad Asghar (pictured). MCS has about seventy faculty members who are responsible for teaching both graduate and undergraduate courses. Dr. Adnan Ahmed Khan heads the Department of Computer Software Engineering.

Department of Computer Software Engineering, MCS, NUST

The Department of Computer Software Engineering at MCS is one of the first Computing Departments in Pakistan which started Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses in Software Engineering. Its Bachelor of Engineering in Software Engineering is accredited by Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC) and so far 20 batches have graduated from this prestigious department. We are also the first Computing Department in Pakistan which started postgraduate programmes in Software Engineering.

About Us

The history of NUST LMS Portal spans over a period of 3 years since it was introduced in NUST Islamabad. NUST School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (SEECS) started using Moodle as LMS from spring of 2008. The deployment of Learning Management System for NUST H-12 campus was approved in May 2010. In a very short time LMS project has moved from being just a pilot project to becoming the integral part of the NUST experience.
